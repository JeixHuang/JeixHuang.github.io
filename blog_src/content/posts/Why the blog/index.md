I write this blog to explore research questions and technical ideas that I find interesting and worth taking seriously, and to contribute back to the open-source community.

It is a place for notes that are useful before they become polished papers: research questions, implementation details, reading traces, open-source experiments, and opinions that become clearer through writing.
