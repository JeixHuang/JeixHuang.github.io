I am a Year-1 Ph.D. student at MMLab, CUHK, working with Prof. Hongsheng Li. My recent work spans multimodal generation, foundation models, reinforcement learning, physical intelligence, and Trustworthy AI. I am interested in building generative systems that are not only expressive, but also grounded in action, feedback, and evaluation. I am always glad to talk with people who care about these questions and like turning research ideas into working systems. 我是香港中文大学 MMLab 一年级博士生，师从 Hongsheng Li 教授。近期研究覆盖多模态生成、基础模型、强化学习、物理智能与 Trustworthy AI。我关注如何让生成式系统不仅更有表达力，也能被行动、反馈与评测约束起来。欢迎和同样关心这些问题、愿意把想法做成系统的人交流。

Multimodal Generation

Unified generation across language, vision, audio, and video.面向语言、视觉、音频、视频的统一生成。

02

Physical AI

Reinforcement Learning
